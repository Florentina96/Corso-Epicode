Lake Braies

Lake Braies, known as the "Pearl among Dolomite lakes", is located in a unique position in the Braies Dolomites.

The origin of Lake Braies - "Pragser Wildsee" in German, "Lago di Braies" in Italian - is referable to a reserrvoir dam, consequence of mudflow.

If you prefer pleasant walks, you can walk around Lake Braies (4km). During a romantic rowing boat trip you can enjoy the bauty of the fairytale - like sceneryy - but only savages will dare to jump into the coolwaters.

  • altitude: 1,469 m a.s.l.
  • Surface: 31 hectares
  • Depth: max. 36m
  • Categoty: mountain lake
